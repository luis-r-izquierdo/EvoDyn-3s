EvoDyn-3s



EvoDyn-3s is a Mathematica computable document designed to analyze evolutionary dynamics in 3-strategy games. To run EvoDyn-3s, you may use the free Wolfram CDF Player or Mathematica. If you use EvoDyn-3s in a publication, you can cite our accompanying paper:

Izquierdo, L.R., Izquierdo, S.S. & Sandholm, W.H. (2018) "EvoDyn-3s: A Mathematica Computable Document to Analyze Evolutionary Dynamics in 3-Strategy Games", SoftwareX, 7, pp. 226-233.
